Bot Market Crashing
Recently due to popular sneaker websites shifting away from first come first serve, they have changed the game in order to prevent botting on their websites. Most popular sites such as yeezy supply, adidas and Nike have all opened waiting rooms. The waiting room is open for an hour, whoever is in the waiting room has a random chance to get the shoes, how fast you join the waiting room is not a factor. Due to these changes the majority of current sneaker bots have lost over half their value.

Overview
To solve this problem we have completely changed the project to be an Nft Racing site. Players can purchase cars from the website, which are ntfs and can use them to race in our game. 10 players will chip in a small fee and the 1st, 2nd and 3rd place winners will receive their part of the prize pool. All payments will be made using Ethereum or our own HBC Crypto. See more details on our Projects page.
